About Me - The Long Story

I am a self-taught programmer with a primary focus on Python and C/C++. I started to learn to code when I was daytrader on CME derivate exchange, as I wanted to automate some of my strategies.

Over time I turned to code more and started dedicating more time to it. Compared to the markets, it had one huge advantage - it was deterministic. The markets are the opposite - they are chaotic. There is no rule of what works and what does not. No one tells you what is correct and what is not. I have been trading and working for very large brokerage companies, and everywhere it was the same. No one knows anything. Everyone is just guessing. This fact became much more clear when I built a random walk AOS - a system trading utterly randomly, disregarding market data - comparing its results to people who strictly followed a "properly tested trading plan". It showed up that the AOS had equal or better results. What a terrifying news!

Nevertheless, I will be forever somehow attracted and involved in the world of financial markets. It is an extremely challenging environment and I seem to like to challenge myself. Sometimes I will make a post about it here on this blog. I still keep an eye on the charts, yet I don't do fulltime daytrading anymore. When I was into that, I was able to spend 10 hours/day watching ticking charts. What a tremendous waste of time! I have coded several studies and trading systems in ACSIL C++, and plan to share some know-how since there is virtually no information about this framework.

Besides trading and coding, I enjoy yoga, meditation, ayurvedic therapy, and all these mystical things that are hidden inside. I am very attracted to Hindu scriptures (I have studied religion at Charles University by the way) and eastern mythology, in general, is something very close to me. In the future, I will definitely make some project devoted to it.

Another of my passion is music. I always had a musical taste somehow different from the mainstream and have been collecting hard-to-find albums from my youth. I decided to build an online radio streamer dedicated to ambient which a very particular genre of electronic music. You can have a listen to it here if you want to undergo a unique form of sonic therapy.
